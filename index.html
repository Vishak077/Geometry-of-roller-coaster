Geometry of a Roller Coaster

Roller coasters use geometry to create safe and exciting rides. Curves, slopes, and loops are designed using mathematical equations.

Important Shapes Used

  • Straight lines – lift hills and brakes
  • Parabolas – hills that give airtime
  • Circles – early loop designs
  • Clothoids – modern smooth loops
